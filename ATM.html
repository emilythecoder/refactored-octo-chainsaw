All About ATM

What is ATM?

ATM stands for Applied Technology Magnet and you can take the 3 year program at Bancroft Middle School. It gives students a chance to learn the importance of computures. You learn so many things, such as photoshop, typing, code, etc. It's a really fun program.

In 6th grade, we learned how to type and make posters.

In 7th grade we learned photoshop and Sketchup

In 8th grade, we are learning code in html and css.
